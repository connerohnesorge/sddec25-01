\textbf{Optimization Achievements:}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{5x Throughput Improvement:} Reduced processing time from 160ms to \textasciitilde33.2ms per 4 frames through pipelined architecture
    \item \textbf{Accuracy Preservation:} Maintained 98.8\% IoU despite aggressive optimization
    \item \textbf{Resource Efficiency:} Sequential DPU scheduling prevents algorithm starvation while maximizing utilization
    \item \textbf{Memory Optimization:} Efficient buffer management within 4GB constraint
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.5em}

\textbf{Model Training Performance:}

Advanced training infrastructure achieved significant improvements:
\begin{itemize}
    \item 15x speedup over CPU baseline (50 minutes vs. 12 hours for 50 epochs)
    \item Multi-component loss function combining boundary-aware cross entropy, generalized dice loss, and surface loss
    \item GPU-optimized training pipeline with batched operations
    \item Comprehensive augmentation for robustness to lighting variations
\end{itemize}
